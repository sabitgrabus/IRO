About us

International Mobility (IRO) is the official department of the International University of Sarajevo (IUS).
Primarily in charge of implementing its international educational policy and promoting the University abroad. The activities of the IUS International Relations Office include:
initiation and processing of the cooperation agreements,promoting and implementing the exchange of students, academic and administrative staff through Mevlana, Erasmus+ and other exchange programs,international admissionother activities in line with IUS strategic goals.
