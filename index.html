About us

International Relations Office (IRO) is the official department of the International University of Sarajevo (IUS).
Primarily in charge of implementing its international educational policy and promoting the University abroad. The activities of the IUS International Relations Office include:
initiation and processing of the cooperation agreements,promoting and implementing the exchange of students, academic and administrative staff through Mevlana, Erasmus+ and other exchange programs,international admissionother activities in line with IUS strategic goals.

Our Services

International admission

Thank you for your interest in our University. We currently welcome applications for the 2019-2020 academic year. We seek to strengthen our academic and cultural diversity with more international students.Esentially, all our study programs are fully provideed in English language, accredited through the ENQA member AQ Austria.
For more information contact us: admission@ius.edu.ba

Student mobility

Study exchange programs are designed in a way to give the opportunity to students and academic staff to study, teach and do research at a university abroad. Students pay no tuition fee to the host university while participate in the Erasmus+ and Mevlana exchange programmes.
For more information contact us: mobility@ius.edu.ba
